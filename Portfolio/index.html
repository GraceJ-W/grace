About Me

I’m a second year at Northwestern University studying Journalism in the Medill School. I’m from Philadelphia but have loved exploring Chicago in my free time. I am the styling director for STITCH, Northwestern’s fashion magazine, as well as writing film reviews for The Daily Northwestern and Dj-ing for WNUR’s rock and jazz shows. Music

For as long as I can remember, music has been a driving and structuring passion in my life, both in the classroom and in my extracurricular life. At Northwestern University, I hostboth the Rock and Jazz shows at WNUR, the university’s radio station, incorporating music into my week as a time to appreciate great artistry amidst a rigorous academic calendar. As a DJ, I am involved in all aspects of making the radio programs happen, from on-air broadcasting to creating programming, and operating soundboards. Finally, from a personal standpoint, I am an avid vinyl collector and try to attend concerts in Chicago whenever I can.

My Top 100 Songs!

Though I’ve eschewed the type of ranking publications like Pitchfork or Rolling Stone typically does, I’ve gathered what are, in my opinion, the 100 best songs. I’ve decided to keep the options to “pop music” — which I am classifying as most music that's not jazz, classical or showtunes.

The attached playlist above of my "Top 100" songs was embedded with a Spotify-made link. To embed an interactive playlist from Spotify, after making a playlist I selected the "Share" option. From there you can select one of two options, "Copy link" or "Embed." The "Embed" option gave me copy-and-paste-able code that I put right into VS Code. Those same steps can be followed any time you want to embed a playlist from Spotify.

Film
I’ve always desired to watch and learn more and do deep dives into director's work, as well as understanding different methods for storytelling and plot development with television. In my first year at Northwestern, I secured a press pass to the Chicago International Film Festival and interviewed Oscar-winner, Emerald Fennell, about her film Saltburn. I have since penned more reviews and had the privilege to interview director Tim Mielants earlier this fall during the festival.
